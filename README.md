# Discover the Flavors of Thailand

## Tom Yum Goong: A Spicy Thai Delight

Tom Yum Goong is a classic Thai soup renowned for its bold flavors and aromatic spices.  
This hot and sour soup features a tantalizing blend of lemongrass, galangal, and kaffir lime leaves,  
combined with succulent shrimp.

> *‚ÄúThe key to a delicious Tom Yum Goong lies in the balance of flavors ‚Äì sweet, sour, salty, and spicy.‚Äù*  
> ‚Äî **Chef Somchai**

---

## Ingredients

- **Lemongrass**: 2 stalks  
- **Thai basil**: 1 cup  
- **Kaffir Lime Leaves**: 3 leaves  
- **Shrimp**: 500g  

---

## Preparation

1. *Simmer* the broth for 10 minutes.  
2. Add lemongrass, galangal, and kaffir lime leaves.  
3. *Stir* in shrimp and *cook* until pink.  
4. *Season* with fish sauce, lime juice, and chili paste.  
5. *Garnish* with Thai basil and serve hot.  

---

## Cooking Tips

- Use 1/4 cup of fish sauce for authentic Thai flavor<sup>*</sup>  
- Add 1 tablespoon<sub>tbsp</sub> of chili paste for signature heat
